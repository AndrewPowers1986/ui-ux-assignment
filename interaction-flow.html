Interaction Flow

  • User: Navigates to web address www.reddit.com
  • System:
    • The Reddit Home page appears.
    • Beebop Rocksteady is already logged in because he has visited Reddit on this device before.
    • The main page displays recent or upvoted threads from Reddit groups he has acknowledge interest in.
    • A search form is clearly visible at the top of the page, next to the Home button, and followed by various tools, options, and settings - which displays his user name in the far right corner.
  • User: Types into the search bar: "middle earth reading list" and hits the Enter key.
  • System:
    • The exact search query is displayed in bold just below the Toolbar.
    • On the page below are three tabs: Best Results, Posts, Communities and users.
    • Best Results is currently selected.
    • Below are a list of Reddit communities related to Lord of the Rings, JRR Tolkien, and the Elder Scrolls.
    • Below that are links to posts by users; the first of which reads: "I want to start my journey of reading middle earth lore, is there a list with books that i should read?"
  • User: Clicks the first post.
  • System:
    • A thread from the r/tolkienfans community that has been archived follows.
    • The very first response contains the most relevant response: "Check out the sidebar for FAQ's and this post about reading order.
  • User: Clicks the link.
  • System:
    • A thread from the r/lotr community titled "Recommended Reading for Tolkien Fans" opens in a new tab, followed by an elaborate post with easy explanations and nice formatting explaining the reading order(s) for Tolkien's Middle-Earth books.
