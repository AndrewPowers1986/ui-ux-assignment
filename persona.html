Persona

Name: Beebop Rocksteady

Technology:

  • PC, iPhone
  • casual user
  • loves the convenience of looking up any curiosity on the go
  • hates when technology tries to help too much (i.e. spell correcting or auto-completing searches with off-the-wall suggestions)
  • somewhat suspicious of being monitored

Attitudes/Needs:

  • uses Reddit to be notified about entertaining articles or posts related to his interests
  • to search, and - failing that - to post, for technical solutions or niche, in-the-weeds questions about video games
  • has a need for information, which he could figure out for himself if he spent enough time, but prefers to learn from the experience of others
  • as much as he hates when technology tries to help too much, he finds unintuitive UI extremely frustrating and will abandon a platform with a steep learning curve
